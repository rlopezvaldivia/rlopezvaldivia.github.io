I was born and raised in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. I studied my M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Astrophysics at the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica in Mexico. Since 2017, I am a postdoctoral fellow at The University of Texas at Austin working at the IGRINS team.

My research interests lie within the stellar physics field, some included topics are:

  • Young Stellar Objects
  • Solar-like stars
  • Atmospheric parameters
  • Chemical abundances
  • Transiting exoplanets

Currently, I am working on the characterization of Young Stellar Objects through high-resolution infrared spectra obtained with IGRINS.
